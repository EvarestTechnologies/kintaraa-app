# P3 Form (Kenya Police Medical Examination Report)

## Files in this directory:
- `Kenya_Police_P3_Form_2024.pdf` - Official P3 form from Kenya Police
- `P3_Form_Guide.pdf` - Complete guide on how to fill the form
- `P3_Form_Sample.pdf` - Sample filled form for reference

## About the P3 Form
The P3 Form is the official medical examination report required by the Kenya Police for documenting injuries in criminal cases. This form is essential for legal proceedings and is free of charge.

## Form Details
- **Official Name**: Kenya Police Medical Examination Report Form P3
- **Updated Version**: 15-page comprehensive form (2024/2025)
- **Issued By**: National Police Service of Kenya
- **Cost**: Free of charge

## Usage
These forms are available offline within the app for immediate access without internet connection.
