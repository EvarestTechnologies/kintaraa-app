# PRC Form - MOH 363 (Post Rape Care)

## Files in this directory:
- `Kenya_MOH_PRC_Form_363.pdf` - Official PRC form from Ministry of Health
- `PRC_Form_Guide.pdf` - Complete guide on how to fill the form
- `PRC_Form_Instructions.pdf` - Step-by-step instructions for healthcare providers

## About the PRC Form
The PRC Form (MOH 363) is the official Ministry of Health form for documenting medical examination and care provided to survivors of rape and sexual violence in Kenya.

## Form Details
- **Official Name**: Ministry of Health Post Rape Care Form (MOH 363)
- **Purpose**: Clinical documentation for rape/sexual violence survivors
- **Use**: Guide for P3 form completion and clinical notes
- **Revised**: 2011 National Guidelines on Management of Sexual Violence

## Usage
These forms are available offline within the app for immediate access without internet connection.
