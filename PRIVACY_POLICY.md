## 1. Introduction

Kintaraa ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of survivors of gender-based violence (GBV). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number (optional), role (survivor/provider)
- **Incident Reports:** Details of reported incidents including description, location, date, and time
- **Messages:** Communications between survivors and service providers
- **Safety Information:** Emergency contacts, safety plans
- **Wellbeing Data:** Mood tracking, journal entries (optional)
- **Voice Recordings:** Audio recordings of incident reports (optional)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app, features used
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates (only when reporting incidents or finding nearby providers)
- **Log Data:** Error logs, crash reports (via Sentry)

### 2.3 Anonymous Reporting
We support anonymous incident reporting. When you choose to report anonymously:
- We do NOT collect or store your personally identifiable information
- Location data is approximate (within 5km radius)
- Service providers cannot see your identity

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- **Provide Services:** Connect survivors with appropriate service providers
- **Safety & Support:** Enable emergency response, safety planning, and wellbeing tracking
- **Communication:** Facilitate secure messaging between survivors and providers
- **Improve Services:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- **Legal Compliance:** Comply with legal obligations and law enforcement requests

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Service Providers
We assign your incident reports to appropriate service providers based on:
- Type of support needed (medical, legal, counseling, etc.)
- Geographic proximity
- Provider availability

### 4.2 Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Cloud Storage:** AWS S3 for evidence and file storage
- **Analytics:** [Analytics Service] for app usage analytics
- **Crash Reporting:** Sentry for error tracking
- **Voice Transcription:** Rork Toolkit for speech-to-text conversion

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or government request, or when necessary to:
- Protect safety of users
- Prevent illegal activities
- Enforce our terms of service

### 4.4 We NEVER:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data for marketing purposes
- Disclose your information to third parties without your consent (except as required by law)

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- **Encryption:** All data transmitted is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Secure Storage:** Tokens and sensitive data stored with device encryption
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access - providers only see assigned cases
- **Audit Logs:** Track all access to sensitive data
- **Regular Security Audits:** Periodic security reviews and penetration testing

## 6. Data Retention

- **Incident Reports:** Retained for 7 years or as required by law
- **Messages:** Retained for duration of active case, then archived
- **Account Data:** Retained until account deletion requested
- **Anonymous Reports:** Retained indefinitely for statistical analysis
- **Deleted Data:** Soft-deleted and purged after 30 days

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to:
- Access all personal data we hold about you
- Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format
- Export your incident reports, messages, and wellbeing data

### 7.2 Correction and Deletion
You can:
- Update your profile information at any time
- Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- Correct inaccurate information

### 7.3 Withdraw Consent
You may:
- Opt-out of location tracking (may limit functionality)
- Disable biometric authentication
- Revoke provider access to your information
- Delete your account
